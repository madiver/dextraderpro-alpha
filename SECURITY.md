# Security Policy

## Supported Builds

Security reports are accepted for currently distributed alpha builds in this repository.

## How to Report a Vulnerability

Do not open public GitHub issues for security vulnerabilities.

Please report privately by email:
- `support@sciencebrew.com`

Include:
- affected version (example: `v0.1.0`)
- platform (`macOS` or `Windows`)
- clear reproduction steps
- impact assessment (what an attacker could do)
- proof of concept, screenshots, or logs (redact sensitive data)

## Response Targets

Target service levels (best effort during alpha):
- acknowledgment within 2 business days
- initial triage within 5 business days
- remediation timeline shared after triage

## Disclosure Guidelines

- Please allow a reasonable remediation window before public disclosure.
- Coordinated disclosure is preferred.
- No bug bounty is offered at this stage.

## Scope Notes

In scope:
- DexTraderPro alpha application binaries and packaging artifacts
- release distribution and update paths in this repository

Out of scope:
- third-party services and dependencies not controlled by Science Brew LLC
- social engineering or physical access attacks

## Safe Harbor

If you act in good faith, avoid privacy violations/data destruction/service disruption, and give us time to remediate, Science Brew LLC intends not to pursue legal action for your research.
